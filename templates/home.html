The Ocular Disease Recognition Tool

This tool is designed to input fundus images and diagnose an ocular disease an individual is suffering from. A provided pair of fundus images of both the eyes were matched with the trained data of images collected from over a lakh patients. The tool gives an accurate result of an eye disease patient is having.

About Us

Insight Institute of Ophthalmology, A Pune based super specialty eye hospital started in 2015, Insight Institute of Ophthalmology has got reputation of most renowned and trust worthy eye care facility in Bhosari & Chakan area of Pune.Earlier people of Bhosari, Chakan, Moshi and nearby areas had to travel long distance for eye surgeries and treatment but now we have all the facilities and infrastructure available for treating all kind of ophthalmology cases. We have separate IPD and OPD departments.Insight institute ophthalmology is headed by Dr Sameer Raskar and Dr Abhijit Agre along with the team of consultants of various super specialty with well-integrated diagnostic, imaging, surgical and laser system.With blend of technology and well qualified doctors we provide high quality compassionate eye care services such as Cornea, Glaucoma, Cataract, Retina, Oculoplasty, Strabismus.Aim of hospital is to provide affordable treatment without compromising quality of services offered. Our mission is to get worlds best technologies at Insight to serve our patients. With our legacy and goodwill,We foresee ourselves as the best eye care in the city
